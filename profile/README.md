## Fixing Common Payment Snags

Card verification failing: confirm issuer eligibility, billing details, and bank authentication before adding the google pay card again. Contactless not working: turn on NFC, unlock the phone, hold it near the reader longer, and check that google pay contactless payment is supported by the terminal. Online checkout missing: choose merchants that list google pay online or pay with google pay at checkout. Account confusion: verify the active google pay account if multiple Google profiles are signed in. Service questions: use google pay support for transaction concerns, device changes, refunds, or google pay services availability.
